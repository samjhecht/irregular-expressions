<Box>
  I got your message  
  Coming down at the speed of sound.   
  To that I send my reply.   
  And I'll have to disagree.   
  Minds don't have pages.   
  But they do have corners.   
  Oh I believe and have seen  
  How dark they can be.   
  The corners of my mind.   
</Box>
